Pasta Recipes

Whether youre looking for a hearty and meaty pasta or to please all of your guests we have a flexible pasta recipe to satisfy your needs. My vegan pasta salad is guaranteed to impress at all your summer BBQs and picnics! Pasta is coated in a creamy and dreamy roasted red pepper sauce and topped with a herby bread crumb topping for extra fresh flavor for an exquisite, grown-up pasta salad.

INGRIEDIENTS

  • pasta
  • parsley
  • Bread crumbs
  • Capers
  • Lemon
  • Walnuts
  • Arugula
  • Basil
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Garlic
  • Smoked paprika

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. the bread slices on a sheet pan
  2. Add the walnuts in empty spaces in the pan
  3. Tearthe bread into small pieces and add it to food processor
  4. Make the red pepper sauce
  5. Add the roasted red peppers, toasted walnuts and half of the toasted bread crumbs
  6. Blend all of the previous items until a thick paste forms
  7. Pour the sauce over the room temp pasta
  8. Use spatula to coat the pasta with the sauce
  9. Add 3/4 cup of water until its saucy
  10. Add in chopped arugula and other mix ins desired
  11. Toss the mix ins into the pasta
  12. Add herby bread crumbs just before serving
  13. Enjoy!!
